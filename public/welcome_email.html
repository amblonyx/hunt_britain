Treasure Hunt Britain aims to provide fun Treasure Hunts, giving everyone the opportunity to explore our favourite places in the UK, in a more interesting way than using a guidebook.

Here's how it works:

  1. Choose where you'd like to do a treasure hunt from our catalogue
  2. Decide whether you want to play on your phone/tablet, or have a paper booklet to follow
  3. You can get the booklet as a PDF to print yourself, or we can send you one in the post
  4. If several teams are going to compete, order one copy of the hunt for each team

If you have any questions, please visit our FAQ. If you still can't find the answer to your question, fill in our online feedback form and we will get back to you.
